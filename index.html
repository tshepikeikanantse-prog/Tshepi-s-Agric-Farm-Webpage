How is Tshepi's Agric Farm and what it produces

Agriculture is the science and practice of cultivating soil, producing crops, and raising livestock to provide food, fiber, and raw materials.TAF main aim is to supply different orginisations (big ans small retailer). It has aready made parteners with Choppies Distribution Companies, Mr veg Distribution Co, Afro Butchery, other small retailers, neighouring farm and other customers with seedlings that are strong, ready and ready to be transplnted.

Tshepi's farm is practicing different branches of agriculture like Horticulture, Animal husbandry, Crop farming, Poultry farming and Aquaculture. There are differents types of fruits and vegetables grown at TAF like mangoes, oranges, lemon's, banana, Peach, Guava and Pomegranate, Avacado, spinach, carrots, sweet potatoes, potatoes, tomatoes, cuccumber, onions's, peas and green pepper. Their are also domestic animals you can find at TAF such as different kinds of Cattle (cows) breeds like Tswana, Brahman,Simental and Tuli, Goats breeds such as Tswana breed, Boer goat, and Kalahari red breed, Sheep, Rabbits, Dogs and Pigs and also poultry birds like layers and broilers chickens, Tuckeys, Ducks and Goose.

Tshepi's Agric Farm supples it's partners with beef carcas, goat and sheep carcas, fruits and vegetables poultry meat and eggs along with fish of which all they are produced in it's farm.TA farm as we speak is looking at introducing bee keekiping and production honey which would also help during the time when crops and fruits start to produce flowers as bees can help by Entomophily (Insect pollination).
